About me

Experienced marketing professional with a demonstrated history of working across Google product areas to drive business objectives. I come from the startup world and am highly creative, innovative and comfortable working cross-functionally.

UI/UX design

I’m a huge advocate of good design, and know that a company’s website is often a brand’s biggest asset. I enjoy designing, learning, reading and talking about design.

Growth through data

A core passion of mine is understanding why online customers behave the way they do, then testing ways to increase traffic and conversions. I do this in tandem by interpreting data and optimizing CTAs, landing pages, copy, and better understanding what online customers are looking for.

Being outdoors

Growing up in San Diego, California, I have a deep appreciation for nature and the outdoors. Whenever possible, I find ways to get outside and be active – be it riding waves along the coast, trail hiking, or biking, I consider myself a very active person.
